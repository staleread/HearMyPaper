Secure your course project, and only let the instructor listen

HearMyPaper is a cross-platform desktop application that lets students protect their academic work with military-grade cryptography.

Download App

How it works

Three simple steps to secure your academic work

Install the app

Available for Windows, Linux (Ubuntu/Debian), and Arch Linux

Submit confidently

Your work is encrypted locally before being submitted

Instructor unlocks

Verified instructors can access and review submissions securely

Key Features

Military-Grade Security

Advanced encryption ensures your work stays protected end-to-end

Project Management

Create and organize multiple projects with drag-and-drop document upload

Simple & Intuitive Interface

Clean, modern desktop UI with one-click submission and real-time status updates

Audio Preview

Convert PDFs to audio for verification before submission

Submission Tracking

Track submission status, timestamps, and manage multiple versions securely

Group-based access control

Students submit to their course group, and only instructors of that group can unlock and review the work

Why HearMyPaper?

Academic integrity is the foundation of education. HearMyPaper ensures trust, fairness, and integrity in course projects by leveraging advanced cryptography to protect student work.

Our system handles all the complex cryptography under the hood, so you can focus on what matters most — your education.
